Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

IMB Innovation ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at https://www.faithbot.tools and its associated AI-powered tools (collectively, the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Usage and Analytics Data. We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized information about how visitors use our site, including pages visited, time on site, browser type, device type, and general geographic region (country/city level). This data does not identify you personally.

Conversation Inputs. When you interact with AI tools such as FaithBot, the text you enter is transmitted to a third-party AI provider for processing. Please do not submit sensitive personal information (e.g., full name, contact details, financial data) in AI chat inputs.

Voluntarily Provided Information. If you contact us directly (e.g., via email), we may receive your name and email address. We use this only to respond to your inquiry.

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Google Analytics places cookies in your browser to distinguish unique visitors and track sessions. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or by adjusting your browser's cookie settings.

We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking, or sell any data to third parties.

3. How We Use Information

  • To understand how our tools are used and improve them
  • To monitor site performance and diagnose technical issues
  • To respond to direct inquiries

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than operating and improving the Services.

4. Third-Party Services

Our Services rely on the following third parties, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics – website analytics (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Third-party AI providers – AI language model processing for tools such as FaithBot

5. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' standard retention settings (26 months by default).

6. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through our site, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

We do not collect data on a personal basis and have no means of tying any usage to a specific individual. All analytics data is aggregate — there is no way for us to identify who visited the site or what any individual user said or did. As a result, we cannot fulfill requests to access, correct, or delete data tied to a specific person, because no such linkage exists. If you wish to stop contributing to aggregate analytics data, you may opt out of Google Analytics (see Section 2).

8. External Links

Our site contains links to external websites (e.g., imb.org, YouVersion). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when changes were last made. Continued use of our Services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the IMB Innovation Team through the IMB website.
